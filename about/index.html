Hello again! I’m Michelle.

Product Designer based near Vancouver, BC, with four years of experience crafting digital products for consumers and B2B.

💫

I specialize in navigating complexity and ambiguity to create intuitive, inclusive, and high-impact solutions. After several years in Health Tech, I’m excited to explore new industries where thoughtful design can make a difference.

👧

My design journey started when I was a little girl who wanted to create websites about Disney princesses. I wasn’t allowed much screen time, so I drew each page on paper and asked people to “click” on links by turning to the next drawing. I didn’t know it then, but I was doing paper prototyping. I’ve been hooked on creating digital experiences ever since!

🎨

Today, I still bring that same joy, curiosity, and heart to my work, along with a structured, systems-minded approach shaped by my Illustration Honours degree and deepened through my work in product teams. Some of my favourite things are accessibility, collaboration, and continuous learning.

🐈

When I’m not designing, I’m usually out walking with a matcha (or hojicha, or chai…). I also love thrifting , a plot-driven videogame , and hanging out with my very round British Shorthair .

📨

Feel free to reach out at hi@michelledesign.ca to talk about all things design or collaborate on a cool project together!
